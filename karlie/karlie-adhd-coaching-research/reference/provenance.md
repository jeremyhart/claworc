# Provenance & Caveats

- **Source:** the Shimmer ADHD blog (shimmer.care/blog) - a commercial ADHD-coaching company. Content-marketing, not a peer-reviewed or clinical source.
- **Scope harvested:** 156 of 157 substantive articles across the full blog archive (pure-marketing posts - product launches, gift guides, FSA/HSA promos - were excluded).
- **Method:** harvested 2026-06-24 by 35 parallel research workers (264 web fetches), each extracting techniques, every cited source verbatim, stream tags, and safety-gap flags; then deduped and synthesised.
- **Trust level:** DISCOVERY ONLY. No claim here has been corroborated against primary sources yet. The `leads-to-validate.md` file is the to-do list for that verification.
- **Known issues flagged during harvest:** several load-bearing statistics (e.g. "ADHD shortens life expectancy 6.5-11 years"; Dodson's "20,000 critical statements") are widely repeated but often distorted and must be checked against primary literature. Crisis resources in the original corpus were US-centric and frequently absent.
