### 2. Notable Topics & Niches the Corpus Surfaces (make sure Karlie covers these)

- **Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria (RSD)** — heavily represented; treated as a brain-based fight/flight/freeze/fawn response with preloaded, rehearsed coping toolkits. Repeatedly linked to depression/suicidality risk in the literature but rarely with safety framing.
- **AuDHD (ADHD + autism comorbidity)** — prevalence/heritability, contrasting traits (novelty vs. sameness), autism-literate CBT caveat, autistic burnout, meltdowns vs. shutdowns, adult assessment pathways.
- **Masking & unmasking** — full arc: what it is, costs (burnout, eroded self-esteem, missed diagnosis), Green/Yellow/Red zone mapping, cost-benefit triage, staged unmasking; also masking *in relationships*.
- **ADHD tax & money** — impulse spending, time-blindness-driven spending, debt (snowball/avalanche), dyscalculia comorbidity, budgeting friction, financial stress → depression/hopelessness.
- **Menopause / perimenopause & ADHD** — estrogen→dopamine/serotonin decline amplifying symptoms; flagged as an elevated-risk window with a safety gap.
- **ADHD & pregnancy / perinatal** — hormonal symptom swings, medication-continuation nuance, perinatal depression/anxiety (safety gap).
- **Trauma / PTSD connection** — trauma worsens (not causes) ADHD; ~6x PTSD risk; explicit suicidality language with no crisis resource (safety gap).
- **Anhedonia vs. apathy** — ventral-striatum/dopamine framing; notably the **positive model** for safety handling (gives 988/741741 + escalation criteria).
- **Late / adult diagnosis & grief** — especially in women; late-diagnosis grief and internalized ableism; "Is it ADHD or me?" reframing.
- **Cultural & identity dimensions** — Black, Latinx, LGBTQ+/neuroqueer, gender-diverse, and gender-dysphoria intersections with ADHD; minority stress, diagnostic bias, and elevated comorbidity/suicidality in several of these groups.
- **Gender differences & underdiagnosis** — inattentive/internalizing presentation in girls/women; the "hyperactive white boy" stereotype as a driver of missed diagnosis.
- **Hypersexuality / low libido & sexuality** — ADHD-driven vs. compulsive sexual behavior disorder; STI-risk data in women.
- **ADHD-driven anger (esp. fathers)** — emotional dysregulation + household-safety dimension (harm-to-others gap, no de-escalation safety net).
- **Love languages / relationships, neurodivergent-flavored** — parallel play, deep-pressure touch, info-dumping, penguin pebbling, support swapping, love-bombing vs. ADHD intensity.
- **Burnout** — ADHD burnout, autistic burnout, parental burnout, productivity-trap burnout — a recurring throughline worth a dedicated treatment.
- **Enclothed cognition / transition cues, "resparkalizing," reflection/journaling, mindfulness adapted for ADHD** — distinctive, transferable niche techniques.
- **Self-diagnosis (pros/cons)** and **whether coaches need ADHD / coach-fit** — useful for handling clients who arrive self-identified and for coach-matching guidance.
